About Me

I grew up in a small town in Southern Oregon, graduating from Portland State University with my degree in Community Health Studies. Upon graduation I was offered a full time job at the Center for Executive and Professional Education at Portland State where I had many roles to play. Originally I was hired to run support for the program managers at the department but as my boss started to notice the skills I had, she gave me more and more opportunities to develop myself and in turn, develop my career.

My boss eventually saw my passion for my web development through my work and development of our departments website that she sent me to the (now shut down) Portland Code School, where I had amazing instructors that taught the basics of HTML, CSS, GIT, and basic java scripting. Through this bootcamp I realized my potential for being a web developer and ever since it has been my passion and career of choice.

Currently I am taking a full stack web developer boot camp course at Epicodus where I am not only learning HTML, CSS, and Javascript, but will also be learning Ruby and Rails. After my completion of this program in November 2018 my goal is to land my first junior developer job where I can continue to hone my skills and talents as a web developer in the city of Portland and beyond.
